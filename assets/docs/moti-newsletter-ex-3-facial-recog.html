The Mayor‚Äôs Gang Prevention Task Force (MGPTF) aims to provide services including vocational training, counseling, and other programs to reduce gang and youth violence. Like we have mentioned before, the understanding that can come from data is biased towards and influenced by how and what questions are asked, what data is collected/available, and the underlying assumptions and inferences made by researchers, all of which tend to reflect broader societal biases.
